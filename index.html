Process

A structured path from idea to deployed prototype.

The goal is to reduce uncertainty early, test the right things, and leave a system that can be understood.

01

Discover

Clarify goals, users, constraints, interfaces, risks, and the first useful milestone.

02

Prototype

Build the hardware, firmware, connectivity, or dashboard layer needed to test the idea.

03

Validate

Measure behavior, debug edge cases, check reliability, and refine the product direction.

04

Deploy

Prepare dashboards, documentation, handover notes, and next-step recommendations.
